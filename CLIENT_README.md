# Pokemon Card Catalog - v1 Guide

## Getting Started

Your card catalog is live and ready to use. Here's how to manage your inventory.

---

## How to Enter Admin Mode

1. Look for the **gear icon (âš™)** in the bottom-right corner of the screen
2. Click it to enter Admin Mode
3. You'll see "ADMIN MODE" appear at the top of the page
4. Click the **X button** to exit Admin Mode when done

---

## Managing Your Cards

### Adding a New Card

1. Enter Admin Mode (click the gear icon)
2. Click the **"+ Add Card"** button that appears
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Card Name** - e.g., "Charizard VMAX"
   - **Card Image** - Click to upload a photo from your device
   - **Meta Tags** - Add details separated by commas, e.g., "PSA 10, English, Base Set"
4. Click **"Add Card"** to save

### Editing a Card

1. Enter Admin Mode
2. Hover over any card
3. Click the **"Edit"** button that appears
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Marking a Card as Sold

1. Enter Admin Mode
2. Hover over the card you sold
3. Click the **"Sold"** button
4. The card will turn gray with a "SOLD" badge
5. To undo, hover and click **"Unmark"**

### Deleting a Card

1. Enter Admin Mode
2. Hover over the card
3. Click the **X** button (red)
4. Confirm deletion

---

## Filtering Cards

Use the navigation bar to filter your inventory:

- **All** - Shows everything
- **Raw** - Ungraded cards
- **Slabs** - PSA, BGS, CGC graded cards
- **Japanese** - Japanese language cards
- **Sealed** - Sealed products

Cards are filtered based on their meta tags.

---

## Important Notes

**About Your Data:**
- Your card data is saved in your browser's storage
- If you clear your browser data or switch browsers/devices, you'll need to re-add your cards
- For a permanent solution in v2, we can add cloud storage

**Best Image Tips:**
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Portrait orientation works best
- Images are stored directly in the browser
